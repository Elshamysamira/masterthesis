Read on to find out what this is all about ... What are authorised card payments ? Authorised payments can be current card payments that have not yet been booked . Or deposit payments , for example fr a hotel or a rental car . These payments have already been made by you , but are not yet finally booked by the merchant . This can take up a few days , depending on how teh accounting is done . The pre-booked amount is no longer available on your account from the tiyme of payment , as it will be credited later , but it should still be ensured that there is enough money on your account . This reduces the amount available on your card .

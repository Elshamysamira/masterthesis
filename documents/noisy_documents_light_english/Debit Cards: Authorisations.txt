18.11.24 , 19:25 Debit Cards : Authorisations Page 1 of 4 https : //www.sparkasse.at/en/george-help/george/your-products/debit-cards/debit-cards -- preauthorisations Debit Cards : Authorisations Last Article Update 29.04.2024 You have probably already noticed it - you pay something with your debit card , but the amount does not immediately appear in the transaction list , instead it is shown as an authorised payment . Read on to find out what this is all about ... Authorisations are card payments that yu have already made , but which have not yet been finally booked by the merchant . For example , when you bought something with your debit card in a supermarket . This means that although the amount does not yet appear in your transaction overview , it has already been taken into account in the balance and is therefore no longer available . To give you a better overview of your ( authorised ) card payments and the available amount , George shows you the payments that have already been booked , but also the authorised ones .

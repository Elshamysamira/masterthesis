George is only able to execute SEPA direct debits under certain conditions . There are some reasons why somebody would like to set up a SEPA direct debit . For example , if you are a property owner and want to retrieve the rent from your tenants . As the name implies , SEPA direct debits are only available within the SEPA region . Since your account is not automatically enabled to create SEPA direct debits , please talk to your avdisor if you want to set up SEPA direct debit and what requirements you must fulfil . If you want to see your SEPA mandates within George , you can simply click on your respective SEPA mandates as legal requirement The legal legitimization for the SEPA direct debit is the so-called mandae . The SEPA direct debit mandate is the consent of the payer ( i.e . debtor ) to the payee ( i.e . creditor ) to collect the receivalbes by direct debit . The mandate data is transmitted by the creditor to the creditor 's bank before each collection and serves as the basis of information for the banks involved in executing the direct debit .

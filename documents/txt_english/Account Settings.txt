Account Settings
Last Article Update 28.05.2024
George is always here to help you manage your
account settings, also called profile settings, in
the most convenient way. Access them via the
George app or the browser version and find out
everything you need to know about your
account(s).
App
George 
Your Products 
Current Accounts
How to get there
Choose your giro account from the
overview.
Go to "Info".
Here you find your main information and
details about your giro account (such as
your IBAN or your available amount). You
also have the option of sharing your
account information if, for example,
someone needs to transfer money to you.
If you want to access your account
permissions (e.g. regarding your account
history or overdraft), please access them
via the George browser version.

These permissions include the following topics:
Account History
Here you can activate the possibility to watch,
download, print, export and filter your account's
transaction history at any time.
Domestic Transfer
If activated, you are entitled to create transfers
within Austria at any time from your account.
Own Transfer
You are entitled to create own transfers at any
time between your accounts.
Foreign Transfer
This permission entitles you to create foreign
transfers SEPA as well as non-SEPA from your
account.
Agreed Overdraft
Please note
If you revoke certain permissions, only your advisor can
reinstall them.

Notifications
Here you can edit (activate/deactivate) and stay
up to date with your notifications. You will be
informed whenever something important is
going on in or with your account.
George also keeps you updated about your
accounts in other ways, too. For example in your
account statements, where a monthly
statement awaits you. You can also edit your
different types of orders, e.g. standing or sweep
orders, or activate features such as Round Up
Savings any time. Plus, the communication
hub keeps you updated about news and
documents that require some kind of attention
from you or the bank.

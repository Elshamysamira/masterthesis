ATM & Branch
Finder
Last Article Update 04.07.2024
You can manage your finances via the George
app or browser version. It's easy, accessible
and saves you a lot of time. But in some cases
you might need to visit a branch or withdraw
money from an ATM. With the ATM & branch
finder you can find what you're looking for with
just a few simple clicks.
App
George 
Communicate with George 
Get in Touch with Your Bank
Please note
Make sure the location services on your phone are
turned on in order for the ATM & branch finder to work
properly.
How to get there
 
Access George.
Click on the "Contact" icon in the bottom
right corner.
Go to "Branches & ATMs".

Whether you are on holiday or shopping
internationally online – a currency converter
can come in handy. And with George you have
one with you at all times. Try it out.
How to get there
 
George 
Your George Settings 
Your Profile
Tap the profile icon on the top right of your
overview.
Within your profile area you then see the
"Currency Converter".

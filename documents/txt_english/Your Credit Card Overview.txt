Your Credit Card
Overview
Last Article Update 15.01.2024
Your credit card is your flexible payment
method on the go – no matter where you are.
Just look out for your credit card's logo. With it,
you can manage your payments easily and
stressless. You can pay in stores or online and
withdraw cash from an ATM. In the credit card
overview, you get all the important information
about your credit card at a glance.

How to get there
Access George.
In the overview (if not hidden), you will
see your credit card(s).
You will see your balance, available
amount, pre-authorised payments and
your limit.
There, you can also choose "Increase" to
increase your available amount.
You can also access your credit card via
"Your Products" in the main menu.
If you have chosen and clicked on your
card, George will go into detail a bit more.
You, again, have the possibility to increase
your available amount. Or you could
change your card limit.
If you klick "Expand" you'll get even more
details such as daily and weekly cash
withdrawal limits.

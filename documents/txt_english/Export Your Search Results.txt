If you want to export your search results,
download transaction details within a specific
time period or exclude certain transactions
from a download, it is best to use the export
function. This gives you the option of
searching for specific transactions, filtering
and downloading the data. Just as you need it.
 
George makes it easy to store or to share your
search results. The export function provides you
with a file that you can customise to meet your
needs exactly.

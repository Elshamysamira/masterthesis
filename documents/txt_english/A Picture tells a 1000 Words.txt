distinguish it from the rest more easily. So why
not give it a go?

Change or add a picture
Access George.
From your overview, or via the menu item
"Your Products", choose the account or
card for which you'd like to add a new (or
different) picture.
Go to the card/product settings.
Change the picture by clicking on the
camera symbol.
 
Click "Change image".
Choose a picture from your computer.
Click "Save".
 
If you want to delete your picture, you can
also do so via the product or card settings
of your account/card.
To do so, click on your recent picture in
the circle above your account and name.
Choose "Delete".
Alternatively, you can also change the
pictures of accounts and cards directly via
the overview, by clicking on the three dots
at the bottom right of the box. Just click
on "Change image", choose a picture and
save the changes.

You sign standing orders once and George will execute them at the selected time interval . George will display your standing orders under your upcoming orders . And , of course , you can edit them . Sweep Orders Sweep orders are also recurring orders , but are a little different from standing roders . When a sweep order is set up , Gerge will always deduct the money that is above the defined threshold and transfer it to wherever you decide - most probably to your savings account . You sign sweep orders once and George will execute them at the selected time interval . You will see your sweep orders in your upcoming orders and can , of course , also edit them .

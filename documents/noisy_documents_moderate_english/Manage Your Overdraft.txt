General information George Your Products Current Accounts You can not enter an amount below your balance . So if you have a minus of 500 € , you can not set the hoverdraft to 400 € . Always enter the amount in steps of 100 , for example 800 or 1500 . To delete an existing agreed overdaft enter 0.00 € in the amount field . The general conditions ( debit interest rate and annual percentage rate ) that apply to you when you overdraw your account are displayed directly to you during the process .
